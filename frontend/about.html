Our Story

Timeless craftsmanship since 2005

Our Mission

At Luxury Jewels, we believe jewelry should be as unique as the person wearing it. Each piece in our collection is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, using only the finest ethically sourced materials.

We're committed to sustainable practices that respect both people and the planet, while creating heirlooms that will be treasured for generations.

Our Journey

2005

Founded in Nairobi Kenya

Derick Munene opened his first small boutique in the heart of Nairobi, specializing in custom jewelry designs.

2007

First International Client

Our reputation for quality craftsmanship attracted clients from across Africa and beyond.

2013

Sustainable Practices

Committed to 100% ethically sourced materials and environmentally responsible production.

2020

Global Online Presence

Launched our e-commerce platform to share our creations with jewelry lovers worldwide.
