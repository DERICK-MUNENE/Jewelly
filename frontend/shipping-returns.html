Returns & Exchanges

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in their original condition with all tags attached.

  • Returns are free within Nairobi
  • International returns are subject to shipping fees
  • Personalized items cannot be returned
  • Refunds are processed within 5 business days

2

Pack your item securely with all original packaging

3
